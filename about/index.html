About Us

I3XI LLC is a Texas-based technology and creative media company founded in 2026.

The company was established with a long-term vision of creating innovative software, intelligent digital products, creative media solutions, and technology-driven experiences that deliver real value to individuals and organizations.

At I3XI, we believe technology should be practical, accessible, and purposeful. Our work is guided by a commitment to quality, innovation, creativity, and continuous improvement. We focus on building products and services that solve problems, improve experiences, and create opportunities for growth.

Our operations span software development, mobile applications, artificial intelligence, photography, videography, digital media production, and emerging technologies. By combining technical expertise with creative thinking, we strive to develop solutions that are reliable, scalable, and impactful.

What We Do

I3XI LLC develops and manages a growing portfolio of technology and creative ventures. Our work includes software engineering, product development, visual media production, digital content creation, artificial intelligence initiatives, and technology consulting.

Every project is approached with a focus on user experience, performance, simplicity, and long-term sustainability.
