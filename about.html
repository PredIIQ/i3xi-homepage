About I3XI LLC

I3XI LLC is a Texas-based technology, software development, digital media, photography, and videography company founded by Tosin Peter Okunowo.

I3XI LLC was formed in 2026 with a vision to build modern digital products, creative media solutions, and technology-driven brands under one strong identity. The company represents innovation, execution, creativity, and ownership.

What We Do

I3XI LLC focuses on software development, mobile and web applications, digital platforms, online business solutions, photography, videography, visual branding, and creative media production.

The company is building a growing ecosystem of products and services, including apps, media tools, creative platforms, and digital experiences designed for real-world users.

Our Brands and Projects

I3XI serves as the parent brand for several creative and technology projects, including digital applications, AI-powered tools, video editing solutions, fitness technology, sports prediction platforms, communication tools, and future creative ventures.

These projects reflect the company’s long-term mission: to combine practical technology with strong design, useful features, and premium user experiences.

Creative Media

Through photography and videography, I3XI LLC also supports visual storytelling, brand presentation, content creation, and digital media services. The goal is to help people, businesses, and brands present themselves with clarity, confidence, and professional quality.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create digital products and media experiences that are professional, useful, and visually powerful. I3XI LLC exists to build, create, and deliver with purpose.

Leadership

I3XI LLC is owned and led by Tosin Peter Okunowo, a U.S. Army veteran, entrepreneur, software builder, and creative founder committed to building a strong technology and media company from the ground up.
